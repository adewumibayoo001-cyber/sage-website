Everything you need

Your kitchen, finally organised.

Pantry tracker

See exactly what you have, what's about to expire, and what's nearly gone. Fridge, freezer, and pantry all in one screen.

Receipt scanning

Take a photo of your grocery receipt and your pantry updates itself. No typing, no barcode hunting. Just point and done.

Miso, your AI chef

Tell Miso what you feel like eating. It checks your pantry and builds a recipe from what you already have.

Smart shopping list

Built from your low-stock items and recipe ingredients. Tap an item when you pick it up and Fridzo adds it to your pantry on the way home.

Meal planning

Plan the week on Sunday, let Fridzo build the shopping list. Fewer last-minute takeaways, more of the food you actually bought.

Spend tracking

See your weekly grocery spend by category, pulled straight from your scanned receipts. No manual logging required.

This is Miso.
Always hungry to help.

Ask what to cook tonight. Get a full shopping list for the week. Figure out what to do with the chicken that needs using up. Miso knows your pantry and gets better the more you cook.
