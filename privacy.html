Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Fridzo ("we," "us," "our") provides an AI-powered pantry management and meal planning app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Fridzo, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: email address, name, and password (encrypted) when you create an account, including via Google Sign-In.

Profile and preferences: dietary goals, health goals, and calorie targets you choose to provide.

Pantry and shopping data: items you add to your pantry, fridge, freezer, and shopping lists, including data extracted from receipt photos or barcode scans you choose to upload.

Miso AI conversations: messages you send to our AI assistant, Miso, which are processed to generate responses and may be retained to improve the service.

Subscription and payment information: we use RevenueCat and the Google Play Store to process subscriptions. We do not directly collect or store your payment card details.

Usage data: basic app usage and diagnostic information to help us fix bugs and improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core features: pantry tracking, meal planning, shopping lists, and Miso AI chat
  • To process receipt and barcode scans using AI-powered recognition
  • To manage your account, subscription status, and entitlements
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

We share limited data with the following service providers, solely to operate the app:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and backend infrastructure
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — powers Miso's chat responses and receipt/barcode scanning
  • RevenueCat — subscription and entitlement management
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (verification, password reset)
  • Google — app distribution and in-app purchase processing

Each of these providers processes data under their own privacy and security practices, and only receives the information necessary to perform their function for Fridzo.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and app data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your associated data from our systems within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal or fraud-prevention purposes.

5. Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete pantry, shopping, and meal plan data at any time within the app
  • You can delete your account entirely from Settings, which removes your data from our systems
  • You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time. Transactional emails such as password resets cannot be opted out of, as they are required for account security

6. Children's Privacy

Fridzo is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encryption in transit and at rest, and row-level security on our database, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Users

Fridzo is available globally. Your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above when we do. Continued use of Fridzo after changes means you accept the updated policy.
