Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Sage ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data when you use the Sage mobile application.

1. Information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Sage service:

  • Account information — your email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth).
  • Pantry data — item names, categories, quantities, expiry dates, and locations you add to your pantry.
  • Shopping list data — items you add to your shopping list, including those added via receipt scanning or barcode scan.
  • Receipt data — when you scan a receipt, we store the store name, total amount, and individual line items (name, category, price) in order to provide spend tracking features.
  • Meal plan data — meals and plans you create within the app.
  • Usage data — scan counts (for daily limits), subscription status, and basic usage metadata.

2. How we use your information

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Sage service:

  • To power the pantry tracker, shopping list, meal planner, and spend tracking features.
  • To provide AI-powered suggestions via Miso (your AI chef), powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Recipe and shopping suggestions are generated using your pantry contents; your data is sent to Anthropic's API for processing under their privacy policy.
  • To enforce daily scan limits and manage your subscription status via RevenueCat.
  • To allow you to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Third-party services

Sage uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (data stored in EU region).
  • Anthropic — AI processing for Miso chat and receipt scanning features.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and in-app purchase processing.
  • Open Food Facts — barcode lookup for product names and categories (no personal data sent).
  • expo-localization — reads your device's locale for currency formatting only; no data is transmitted.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

4. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen within the app. Deleting your account permanently removes all your pantry items, shopping lists, meal plans, receipts, and account information from our systems within 24 hours.

5. Data security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Supabase with row-level security (RLS) policies that ensure each user can only access their own data. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

6. Children's privacy

Sage is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, or if you have questions about your data, please contact us at privacy@getsageapp.com.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@getsageapp.com.
